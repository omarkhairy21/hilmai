# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2025**

At hilm.ai, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal and financial information when you use our AI personal finance assistant.

## Information We Collect

### Financial Data
- Transaction details (amounts, merchants, categories, dates)
- Receipt images and extracted information
- Voice messages and transcriptions
- Spending patterns and financial queries

### Account Information
- Telegram user ID and username
- Currency and timezone preferences
- Subscription and billing information (processed securely through Stripe)

### Usage Data
- Commands and interactions with the bot
- Feature usage patterns
- Error logs and diagnostics

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide personalized financial insights and recommendations
- Process and categorize your transactions
- Answer your financial questions
- Improve our AI models and service quality
- Send you important service updates (via Telegram)

## Data Storage and Security

- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Database:** Your data is stored securely in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with industry-standard security measures
- **Access:** Only authorized systems can access your data
- **Retention:** We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third parties except:
- **Service Providers:** We use trusted services (Supabase, OpenAI, Stripe) that are necessary to operate hilm.ai
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law or to protect our rights

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account and data
- Export your transaction data
- Opt out of certain data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us via Telegram (@omark4y) or email at support@hilm.ai.

## AI and Data Processing

- We use OpenAI's API to process your messages, voice, and images
- Your data may be used to improve our AI models (in accordance with OpenAI's privacy policy)
- We do not use your data to train third-party models without your consent

## Children's Privacy

hilm.ai is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via Telegram.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us:
- Telegram: [@omark4y](https://t.me/omark4y)
- Email: support@hilm.ai
